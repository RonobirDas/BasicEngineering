I have an Artix 7 from Digilent that allows for breadboard usage, and an Arrow SoCKit which I love, but is grossly overkill for most projects outside of architecture ones. I'd like a simple and cheap setup but with more ALMs or LUTs than what lattice is offering. That and I'm too comfortable with Intel/Altera's setup to really move to something else. Ideally, it being a nonBGA design would be ideal. For price/performance I've found that the Cyclone IV E family to be a good price between $12 for 6k and $35 for 22k. The Cyclone 10 LP is also decent at 25k and much more recent. However the best bang for buck would be the Cyclone V family, where in I can get a consistent ratio of around 700 - 850 LEs/$. The best deal is a 300k LE Cyclone V for <$250, giving you around 1450 LEs/$. The lower priced Cyclone IV are great for one off designs since they are not BGA based.
